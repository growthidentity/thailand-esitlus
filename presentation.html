Arbitrage
What is Arbitrage?
Company's Core Activity - 50% of Revenue
Arbitrage Explained:
Buying currency or crypto at a lower price in one location
and selling it at a higher price in another
Simple Example:
  • Buy a banana at the market for ₱60
  • Sell it on the next street for ₱66
  • Earn ₱6 profit
  • Repeat daily
Crypque Does the Same Thing:
  • Using USDT instead
  • With large volumes
  • Every single day
  • Fully automated
Real Life Example - Currency Exchange
Popular currency exchanger (buy/sell rates):
USD: They buy from you at ₱66.50 → They sell to you at ₱70.30
Difference: ~5.7% profit per transaction

That's arbitrage—buy low, sell high!
